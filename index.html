Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 27, 2026

Recordit! respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information and data when you use the application.

Information Collection

Recordit! does not require user accounts and does not directly collect personal information such as names, addresses, or payment information.

Information you enter into the app, including game sessions, scores, players, images, and related content, is stored locally on your device.

Advertising

Recordit! uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect certain device information and identifiers to provide and improve advertisements, including:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • Interaction data related to advertisements

Users may be asked for permission regarding app tracking in accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services including:

  • Google AdMob
  • Apple iOS Services

These services may process limited technical data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

Most application data is stored locally on your device using Apple's local storage technologies.

Recordit! does not currently provide online account systems or cloud-based personal profile storage.

Children's Privacy

Recordit! is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Disable tracking permissions in iOS Settings
  • Limit ad tracking through your Apple device settings
  • Delete the app and its local data at any time

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be reflected on this page.
